22.8    Further Reading

Application testing and TDD are hot topics, and more on JUnit and Mockito is to find in the book Practical Unit Testing with JUnit and Mockito by Tomek Kaczanowski. For AssertJ it’s useful to study the very good examples that are available online: https://github.com/assertj/assertj-examples/tree/main/assertions-examples/src/test/java/org/assertj/examples.

Another worthwhile task might be to review a more critical look at TDD, for example, inspired by “TDD Harms Architecture” (https://blog.cleancoder.com/uncle-bob/2017/03/03/TDD-Harms-Architecture.html).
